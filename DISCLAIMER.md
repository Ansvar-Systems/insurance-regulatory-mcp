## Recommended Verification Workflow

Before relying on any regulatory information retrieved through this server:

1. **Identify the relevant standard** using this server's search tools
2. **Retrieve the official document** directly from the IAIS, NAIC, or Lloyd's portal
3. **Confirm the version and effective date** — check whether the document has been revised or superseded
4. **Read the full original text** — do not rely solely on summaries or excerpts
5. **Consult qualified legal or compliance counsel** for interpretation and application to your specific facts
6. **Check national implementation** — ICP requirements are applied by each jurisdiction's supervisor and may vary significantly from the IAIS baseline
